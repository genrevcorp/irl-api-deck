01 — The problem01 — 問題

Software runs every business. It just can't see. 軟體管理著每一家公司，卻看不見現場。

The ERP knows the orders, the bookings, the inventory. It doesn't know whether table 4 is actually cleared, court 2 is actually occupied, or bird #1042 stopped eating this morning. ERP 知道訂單、預約、庫存，但它不知道：4 號桌收乾淨了沒？2 號球場現在有人在打嗎？編號 1042 的雞，今天早上是不是不吃飼料了？

GAP·01

The last 10 meters are manual最後一哩路，還是靠人工

The physical world enters software through a human typing — late, wrong, and expensive. Most of it never gets entered at all. 現場的狀況要進到系統，得靠人手動輸入——慢、容易錯、又花人力。而更多時候，根本沒人輸入。

GAP·02

AI cameras exist — as closed systems市面上有 AI 攝影機，但都是封閉系統

Camera-AI vendors sell complete, sealed products: their screen, their app, their login. Your own software can't tap into what their cameras see. 廠商賣的是整套自己的產品：自己的螢幕、自己的 App、自己的帳號。你原本的系統接不進去——你多了一組密碼，卻沒有多一雙眼睛。

GAP·03

"Person detected" is worthless光是「偵測到人」，沒有用

Generic detection is everywhere already. What a business actually acts on is meaning: "table ready", "this bird may be sick", "court just freed up". 通用的偵測功能到處都有。企業真正需要的，是跟生意直接相關的訊息：「這桌可以帶位了」、「這隻雞可能生病了」、「球場空出來了」。

02 — The product02 — 產品

One webhook. The real world, structured. 現場發生什麼事，你的系統馬上知道。

We don't sell "person detected." We sell the event your business actually runs on — delivered with the same reliability mechanics Stripe uses for payment notifications: verified, versioned, never lost, never duplicated. 我們賣的不是「偵測到人」這種陽春訊號，而是你的生意真正用得上的事件通知——格式穩定、來源可驗證、不漏接、不重複，可靠程度比照金流通知的等級。

In plain words白話來說

A "webhook" is like an automatic text message between computer systems. The moment something happens on-site, your software instantly receives a short note saying exactly what happened — no one has to watch a screen, no one has to type anything in. 「webhook」就像系統跟系統之間的 LINE 訊息——現場一有狀況，你的軟體立刻收到一則寫清楚「發生了什麼事」的通知。不用有人盯著螢幕看，也不用有人打字輸入。

{
  "schema":    "irl.event.v1",
  "type":      "pet.cough_cluster",   // or table.ready · court.occupied · flock.activity_drop
  "severity":  "critical",
  "subject":   { "kind": "pet", "id": "momo" },
  "detection": { "detector": "frequency_window", "confidence": 0.81 },
  "enrichment":{ "status": "confirmed", "confidence": 0.93,
                 "description": "Cough frequency above per-pet baseline." },
  "media":     { "clip_url": "https://…signed…" }
}

The block above is everything your software receives — what happened, to whom, how confident the AI is, with the video clip attached. Your developers connect it in an afternoon, and you never touch an AI model. 上面這份就是你的系統收到的全部內容：發生什麼事、發生在誰身上、AI 有多確定，還附上影片連結。你的工程師一個下午就能串接好，而你完全不用碰 AI 模型。

03 — Architecture03 — 運作方式

Gate cheap. Enrich smart. Deliver clean. 小 AI 顧現場，大 AI 做判斷，結果直接進系統。

01 · edge

Always-on watcher24 小時顧現場的小哨兵

A $30-class sensor (camera + radar) with a small built-in AI keeps watch around the clock: did something appear, move, make a sound? It's cheap enough to never turn off. 一台約千元台幣的感測器（攝影機＋雷達），內建小型 AI，全天候注意現場：有東西出現嗎？有動靜嗎？有聲音嗎？因為夠便宜，可以一直開著。

COST ≈ 0 · ALWAYS WATCHING
02 · cloud

Smart AI reads the moment聰明的 AI 看懂狀況

Only the moments that look interesting are sent to a powerful cloud AI that genuinely understands images. Supporting a new industry is a configuration change — not a newly trained model. 只有「疑似有事」的片刻，才會送到雲端，讓看得懂影像的大型 AI 判斷一眼。要支援新的產業，改一份設定就好——不用重新訓練模型。

PENNIES / EVENT · BUDGETED
03 · api

Result lands in your software結果直接送進你的系統

Fixed format, verified sender, automatically retried — never lost, never duplicated. Plus an actions channel back: play a sound, send a warning. It can see, and it can act. 判斷結果立刻送進你的軟體：格式固定、可驗證、不漏接、不重複。還能反過來下指令：播放聲音、發出提醒——不只看得到，還能出手。

irl.event.v1 · 2-WAY

Why split it this way? Because watching is cheap and understanding is expensive. This is exactly how Verkada — the largest cloud security-camera company in the US — affordably processes ~1 billion hours of video a month: cheap detection on the camera filters first, the costly AI only looks at what matters. We apply the same physics to every industry, as an open API. 為什麼要這樣分工？因為「看著」很便宜，「看懂」很貴。美國最大的雲端監控公司 Verkada，就是靠這套「前端先過濾、AI 只看重點」的做法，才能用合理成本處理每月約 10 億小時的影像。我們把同一套邏輯用到保全以外的各行各業，而且做成開放的 API。

07 — Business model07 — 商業模式

They keep the customer. We're the senses inside. 我們不直接賣給店家，我們讓店家用的軟體變聰明。

We sell to the software companies that already serve each industry — the ERPs and SaaS that own the customer relationship. Embedding us gives their product a flagship feature nobody else has; for us, it's recurring revenue without a direct sales army. 我們的客戶是已經在服務各行各業的軟體公司（ERP、SaaS）。對他們來說，接上我們，產品就多了一個別人沒有的功能；對我們來說，這是不需要養業務大軍的長期收入。

In plain words白話來說

Think of Google Maps inside Uber: you never "buy Google Maps," but every ride uses it. The restaurant keeps using the software it already has — that software quietly plugs into us, and suddenly it can see the dining room. 就像 Uber 裡面用的是 Google 地圖——你不會直接「買 Google 地圖」，但每趟車都在用它。餐廳老闆繼續用原本的系統，只是那套系統背後接上了我們，從此看得見現場。

REV·01

Hardware, one-time硬體：一次性收費

Commodity sensors at a fair hardware margin. No camera-company ambitions — the device is just the delivery vehicle for events. 平價感測器，賺合理的硬體毛利。我們不是要做攝影機公司——硬體只是把事件送出來的載體。

REV·02

API, per device / monthAPI：按裝置數收月費

The software company pays per connected device for the event stream — a small slice of the value of the feature they ship on top of it. 軟體公司按接上的裝置數量付月費，取得事件串流。跟他們靠這個功能多賺的錢相比，這筆費用只是零頭。

REV·03

Industry packs產業設定包

Premium event types (health-critical alerts, compliance monitoring) priced as pack tiers. New packs ship as configuration — our marginal cost approaches zero. 進階事件（健康警示、合規監測）以設定包分級收費。新增一個產業只要寫設定檔——我們的邊際成本趨近於零。

08 — Defensibility08 — 護城河

Models commoditize. Hardware commoditizes. Verticals don't. AI 會變便宜，硬體也會。懂一個產業，不會。

M·01

Data flywheel資料飛輪

Every deployed sensor labels its industry's hardest cases. A per-subject baseline — what's a normal cough rate for this cat — can't be bought off the shelf. 每一台裝機中的感測器，都在累積該產業最難判斷的案例。像「這隻貓平常咳幾聲才算正常」這種個體基準，市面上買不到。

M·02

Deployment robustness裝得起來、跑得穩，才是真功夫

The unglamorous 5% — mounting, lighting, dust, flaky Wi-Fi, false-alert tuning. It's where every horizontal platform died. We do it per industry, once, deeply. 安裝角度、光線、灰塵、不穩的 Wi-Fi、誤報的調校——最不起眼的 5%，正是過去所有通用平台陣亡的地方。我們一個產業一個產業，把它做透。

M·03

The contract devs build on合作夥伴離不開的格式

Once a software company ships features on irl.event.v1, switching costs are theirs to fear, not ours. 當軟體公司基於我們的事件格式 irl.event.v1 推出功能之後，要換掉我們的成本，就變得非常高。

M·04

Swappable perceptionAI 進步是順風，不是威脅

The perception layer is built to be swapped — specialized models today, stronger models tomorrow. Every AI breakthrough lowers our cost and raises our accuracy. 感知層的設計就是可以整顆換掉——今天用特化的小模型，明天換更強的新模型。AI 每進步一次，我們的成本就更低、判斷就更準。

09 — Landscape09 — 市場格局

Everyone's at the two ends. The middle is open. 所有人都擠在兩端，中間是空的。

Verkada · Ambient.ai · Coram
Own one vertical (security), sell closed end-products direct to enterprises. No embeddable API for other software. They validate the architecture — and leave the API lane empty. 深耕單一產業（保全），整套產品直接賣給大企業，不開放其他軟體串接。他們證明了這套架構可行——也剛好把 API 這條路空了下來。
Azena † · Azure Percept † · SenseCraft
Horizontal "camera app-store" platforms. Dead or unadopted — platforms-for-everyone serve no one. The graveyard that shaped our vertical-first sequencing. 想做「攝影機 App 商店」的通用平台，不是收掉就是沒人用——想一次服務所有產業的平台，最後一個產業都服務不好。這片墳場，正是我們堅持「一次做深一個產業」的原因。
Roboflow · Clarifai · Lumeo
Dev-tools: they sell shovels to teams who build their own pipelines. The buyer must still create the meaning. We sell the gold, not the shovel. 賣工具給工程師，讓買家自己蓋系統——「意義」還是得買家自己做出來。我們不賣鏟子，直接賣挖好的金子。
Butlr
Closest analog — sensing API embedded in third-party software, but occupancy-only (counting people with radar). Proves the business model works; stops short of business meaning. 跟我們最像的公司——把感測 API 嵌進第三方軟體，但只做「空間裡有幾個人」這一件事。他們證明了這個商業模式行得通；我們把它再往前推一步，做到產業意義。
IRL API
Embeddable, multi-sensor, business-meaning events, sold to software companies. The combination no one occupies today — in a lane many will enter soon. Speed and industry depth decide it. 可嵌入、多感測器、具產業意義的事件，賣給軟體公司。這個組合目前沒有人做——但這條賽道很快就會熱起來，勝負就在速度跟產業深度。
